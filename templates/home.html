ABOUT US

Founded in June 2015 by Mr. Vijay Singh Senger, Gym Lounge India began its journey with just 8 dedicated staff members and a vision to revolutionize fitness culture in Gujarat.

Today, that vision has grown into reality — with 24 outlets across the state and a strong team of 300+ fitness professionals, Gym Lounge India proudly stands as the largest gym chain in Gujarat.

Over the years, our brand has become synonymous with quality fitness, innovation, and excellence. Our grand gym inaugurations have witnessed visits from renowned celebrities, adding to our growing reputation and community presence.

With numerous prestigious awards recognizing our excellence in fitness and customer satisfaction, Gym Lounge India has firmly established itself as the No. 1 gym brand in Gujarat.

Guided by the visionary leadership of Mr. Vijay Singh Senger, we are now gearing up for our next big milestone — expanding our presence across India. Our mission is to empower individuals nationwide to live healthier, stronger, and more confident lives through fitness.
